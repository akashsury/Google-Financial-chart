Financial Summary

This tool is created for displaying the contents of the revenues and profits of tech giant 'Google' to get quick mental picture about the 13 years(2002 - 2015) financial performance of the company. This will be useful for investors or anyone who are interested to explore the financial history and to get quick grasp the company performance quickly. The revenues and profits are plotted here. Using this bar chart, the relationship between revenues and profits can be viewed simultaneously.
